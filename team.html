Our Team

Not career advisors. Recent applicants.

We went through the same pressure, uncertainty, and decision-making — and matched successfully into competitive specialties. We didn't learn this from the sidelines. We learned it by building our own applications, making mistakes, adjusting in real time, and ultimately getting the outcome we were aiming for.

everything we share comes from what actually mattered, what didn't, and what we wish we knew earlier.

I'm an incoming Dermatology Resident and a graduate of UMass Chan Medical School who recently went through the residency application process.

A big part of my success came from building a strong research portfolio, learning how to network effectively, and performing at a high level on away rotations. I learned quickly that it is not just about working hard — it is about being strategic with your time and how you present yourself.

I work with students on application strategy, research direction, and personal statements. I help you turn your experiences into a clear and compelling story that programs take seriously.
